Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

TextCatch ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website textcatch.app and provides a chat-to-text lead-capture service. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

When you interact with our chat widget or forms, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • The message or question you submit
  • Basic technical data such as the page you contacted us from

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry, follow up by text message (SMS) or email, and provide the service you requested. We do not use it for unrelated purposes.

SMS / text messaging

By submitting your phone number through our chat widget or forms, you consent to receive text messages (SMS) from TextCatch related to your inquiry. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

No mobile information will be sold or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Phone numbers and SMS consent are never shared with third parties. Information may be shared only with service providers (such as our messaging provider) strictly as needed to deliver the service, and as required by law.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the vendors that operate our service (for example, our SMS and email delivery providers) and only to the extent necessary to provide it, or when required by law.

Data retention

We retain the information you submit for as long as needed to respond to and serve you, and to meet our legal obligations. You may request deletion of your information at any time (see below).

Your choices

  • Opt out of texts anytime by replying STOP.
  • Request access to or deletion of your information by contacting us.

Contact us
